Track your goals. Track your Life.

A simple app that lets you track your goals and lets see over time how all of the daily tasks you perform lead to achieving those goals.

As a hybrid between data and motivation, we try to provide you a stripped down interface to motivate yourself with the tools you need to succeed.

This was a simple idea that grew to a web app & in the future mobile application, so if you have suggestions please forward them along.
